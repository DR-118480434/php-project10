Music

My preferred genre of music is hip-hop. Since I was young i always had an interest in the genre and that interest has grown into a passion. I spend most of my days listening to music on Spotify and feel annoyed when I don't spend part of my day relaxing and listening to music. Rap isn't all that I listen to either as I often find myself dipping into other various genres from time to time.

Some of my favourite artists to listen to include Eminem, Kanye West, 2Pac, XXXTentacion and Kendrick Lamar. I find these artist's music very inspiring and their discography's are some of the greatest out there in my opinion.

My favourite albums from these artists include Graduation, Me Against the World and Damn.
